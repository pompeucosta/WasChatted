The Wanganui Herald, originally published as The Wanganui Chronicle, is a weekly tabloid newspaper based in Wanganui, New Zealand.  The Wanganui Herald was first published in 1864 as the Wanganui Chronicle, a weekly newspaper. The paper was bought by the NgÄti Porou iwi in 1955 and renamed the Wanganui Herald. The paper is now owned by Fairfax Media.
