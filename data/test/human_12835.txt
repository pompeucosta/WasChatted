August Arthur Petry (1858, Tilleda - 1932  Nordhausen) was a German botanist and entomologist specialising in Microlepidoptera. He was a teacher of philology at the Gymasium in Nordhausen. August Petry was a Member of the Stettin Entomological Society.Ottmar Hofmann honoured his name  in Caryocolum petryi. His collections are, in part, held by the Natural History Museum of Erfurt.
